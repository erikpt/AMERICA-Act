# AMERICA Act
## Your healthcare. Guaranteed.

No networks. No denials. No coverage gaps. Essential care for every American — no matter your job, income, or health history.

---

## What you get

**See any doctor, anywhere.**
No networks. No referrals. No prior authorization. Walk into any licensed provider in the country and get care.

**Coverage that follows you.**
Change jobs, start a business, lose work, retire early — your coverage never lapses. It's yours.

**Mental health, dental, vision, and hearing included.**
Not as add-ons. Not as extras. Built in from day one.

**No surprise bills.**
Providers are paid by the program. Balance billing is illegal. You pay your co-pay and nothing more.

---

## What you pay

Co-pays are based on your income — and capped at **5% of what you earn per year**, no matter what.

| Your income | Doctor visit | Prescription | ER |
|---|---|---|---|
| Under $75,000 | $0 | $10 | $50 |
| $75k – $200k | $20 | $20 | $100 |
| Over $200k | $40+ | $40+ | $200+ |

**Preventive care, vaccines, and kids' visits are always $0.**

---

## What stays the same

- Your doctor (if they're licensed, they're in)
- Health Savings Accounts (HSAs)
- Supplemental insurance for extras you want
- VA, TRICARE, and Indian Health Service benefits

---

## How it's paid for

The U.S. already spends more on healthcare than any country on Earth. This act stops wasting that money on insurance company overhead and redirects it to actual care — backed by a new payroll contribution shared between employers and workers.
